Favorite Three CD Releases:

The Lumineers

This album was recorded in 2011 and released 2012. This is the album that first attracted me to this particular band. This album has som of the best American folks songs.

Cleopatra

This album has many favorites within in. "Ophelia" which has been played on the radio many times is recorded on this album.

The Hunger Games: Catching Fire

This album was released in 2013 for the movie "The Hunger Games". It is the least favorite album of mine from The Lumineers.
